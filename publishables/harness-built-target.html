Components — Who owns what

The model is allowed to be creative. The evidence pipeline is not. The runner owns isolation, tests own classification, the database owns retained records, the evaluator owns interpretation, and the verifier owns publication invariants.

What the system still cannot prove 6 gaps                                                           Target 5 items

These are not roadmap decorations. Each missing control limits a claim the project can safely make.
External audit workflow
No third-party verification of hidden tests, evaluator verdicts, or artifacts. Tier 1 only.
Multi-evaluator agreement
Single evaluator model. No inter-evaluator metric. 10 reviews, 1 evaluator.
Retry strategy variants
Pass@k only re-samples from scratch. No hint-based, progressive, or decomposed retry.
Prompt length analysis
cv prompts are longer. Token profiling not implemented. Can't measure prompt→outcome dose-response.
Gateway confound (North Mini)
North Mini product cells ran through OpenRouter, earlier packs through OpenCode Zen. Cannot separate gateway from pack effects.
Continuous monitoring
On-demand only. No scheduled re-runs when providers update or models change.
