Recommendation Partition CI repair by task type. Mechanical: delegate with clear specs. Business logic: human review.

Prior evidence

This matrix is the latest piece of a larger research program. The original pass@1 study tested 5 models across 4 families (North Mini, Gemma 4 12B/e4b/31B, DeepSeek V4 Pro, Laguna XS.2) — ~600 runs total. The core finding held across all of them: a consistent 20–62 point trust gap. Three scenarios remained unconquered by any model at pass@1. The contract-visible effect was measured across 4 models on ci_forensics and maintenance_value. See paper.html and v11.html for the full multi-model evidence.
